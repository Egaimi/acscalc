About This Tool

Version 1.0

This tool consolidates the validated TIMI (Antman et al., 2000), GRACE (Eagle et al., 2004), and Mehran (Mehran et al., 2004) risk scores to facilitate comprehensive risk assessment in acute coronary syndromes. It is intended for informational purposes only and does not replace clinical evaluation or professional medical judgment.
